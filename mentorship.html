Why should I consult with you?

Why should I
consult with you?

In my 4-year expedition within the design industry, navigating the lanes of freelancing and consulting for in-house designers and art directors, I've come a long way.

The path has been both challenging and rewarding, prompting me to ponder: 'What if I had a mentor?' I believe it would have fast-tracked my journey to this point, perhaps within a year or two.

Driven by this realization, I'm driven to extend a helping hand to fellow creatives. I want to help you in honing your design skills, mastering the art of thriving in this industry.

What's in it for me?

This mentorship journey offers three plans: Basic, Standard, and Premium. Regardless of the plan you choose, you get the 'Ask Me Anything' session, delving into design, business dynamics, career guidance, and beyond.

This mentorship experience is crafted primarily for aspiring to junior and mid-level designers, enriching those with 0-4+ years of experience. However, if you've been in the industry for a while, I can still provide invaluable insights into the business of design and strategies to elevate your pricing.
