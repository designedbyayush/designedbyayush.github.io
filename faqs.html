How long does a project take?

As no two businesses are alike, project timelines come in all shapes and sizes. From quick website audits that can be wrapped-up in an hour to epic year-long brand revamps, the duration of a project hinges on its unique intricacies.

But if you are curious about numbers, a standard website or app redesign typically takes 2-3 months. This routine includes 2-3 weeks of discovery and strategy, 1-2 weeks of research, 2-3 weeks of visual design, and a final 1-2 weeks of testing and evaluation.
