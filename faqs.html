How long does a project take?

Since the scope of the project differs from company to company, so does the deadline. The timeline varies based on the type of service the client needs. From a website audit taking an hour to a full-fledged brand revamp taking around a year, it is almost impossible to estimate a duration without knowing the intricacies of the project.

However, a usual redesigning of a website takes around 1-2 months, which consists of 2-3 weeks of discovery and strategy, 1-2 weeks of research, 2-3 weeks of visual design, 1-2 weeks of testing, and evaluation.
